SICP solutions

Ramón L. Panadés Barrueta

Welcome to my solutions to the exercises in Abelson and Sussmand’s Structure and Interpretation of Computer Programs (SICP). As the design of this website suggests, I have used Racket for the task, with the languages #lang sicp, #lang racket, and #lang lazy.

I am a physicist by training, so there may be some areas where my solutions could be improved. If you find any, please help me avoid embarrassment by submitting a pull request. If you’d like to compare my solutions to those of others, you can visit the Scheme wiki and other resources linked there.
